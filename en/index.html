Who is this for?

Any builders (technical or non-technical) who are interested in building a successful educational community within NEAR eco-system.

What is this Playbook about?

A Deep Dive Into NEAR Hispano Case Study

NEAR Hispano has educated over 600 developers across the South American continent. By studying its success, we will develop a guide that will facilitate its replication in different continents.

NEAR Hispano Case Study: How to build a successful educational community

By studying the success of NEAR Hispano, we developed a guide for regional leaders to replicate its success in their own countries.
Through in depth interviews and expert consultations we captured the key principles that will help regional leaders kickstart their own educational community.
